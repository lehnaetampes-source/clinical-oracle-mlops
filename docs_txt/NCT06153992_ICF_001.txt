【Why should this study be conducted?】By constructing and improving the


[NarrativeText] (page 3)
clinical trial system of our project team, we collect clinical data from different


[NarrativeText] (page 3)
disease rehabilitation processes, test and verify the stability, rehabilitation effect,


[NarrativeText] (page 3)
and feasibility of promoting the intelligent isokinetic training and evaluation


[NarrativeText] (page 3)
system to the market. At the same time, we accumulate important clinical data.


[NarrativeText] (page 3)
【 How to conduct this study 】 This study is a single center randomized


[NarrativeText] (page 3)
controlled study. You will be randomly assigned to the treatment group, the


[NarrativeText] (page 3)
control group, and the two treatment groups to receive conventional rehabilitation


[Title] (page 3)
recommendations on rehabilitation treatment


[Title] (page 3)
in the 2017 edition of


[Title] (page 3)
the


[Title] (page 3)
"Guidelines for Early Rehabilitation Treatment of Stroke in China";


[Title] (page 3)
Intelligent


[NarrativeText] (page 3)
isokinetic therapy adds isokinetic muscle strength training to the lower limb


[Title] (page 3)
flexion and extension muscles of hemiplegia in the above treatments. The


[NarrativeText] (page 3)
intelligent portable isokinetic tester of this project team was used for isokinetic


[NarrativeText] (page 3)
training of the affected knee flexor and extensor muscles. The patient's seat


[NarrativeText] (page 3)
backrest was adjusted to 85 °, and the waist and shoulder cross straps were fixed.


[NarrativeText] (page 3)
The proximal thighs of the test subjects were fixed with nylon buckles, the lateral


[NarrativeText] (page 3)
condyle of the femur was the axis, and the length of the arm was 12 on the scale.


[NarrativeText] (page 3)
The distal ankle of the lower leg was fixed above the ankle. Before training, passive


[NarrativeText] (page 4)
joint movements were performed on the knee and ankle joints to avoid joint


[NarrativeText] (page 4)
damage, And conduct three low resistance warm-up exercises. Choose 60 °/s, 90 °


[NarrativeText] (page 4)
/s, and 120 °/s angular velocity isokinetic training based on the patient's specific


[NarrativeText] (page 4)
each cycle, and 2 minutes of rest between each cycle. Train for 4 cycles according


[NarrativeText] (page 4)
to the patient's tolerance, with an appropriate amount of training to cause


[NarrativeText] (page 4)
per training session). Train each muscle group once a day, 5 days a week, for a total


[NarrativeText] (page 4)
of 3 weeks. The control group received 110 minutes of daily training, while the


[NarrativeText] (page 4)
observation group received 135 minutes of daily training. Traditional isokinetic


[NarrativeText] (page 4)
therapy has the same treatment parameters as the intelligent isokinetic group in


[Title] (page 4)
this project group.


[NarrativeText] (page 4)
During the treatment and follow-up period, researchers will use relevant


[NarrativeText] (page 4)
clinical observation tables to collect all data that needs to be observed after 3


[NarrativeText] (page 4)
weeks of treatment, and finally summarize the data for statistical analysis.
